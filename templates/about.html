About Stone Edge Capital

Founded in 2023 by Shantanu Jha and Kalash Mendole, Stonedge Capital is an independent, employee-free hedge fund focused solely on public equity markets. Operating with a lean, high-agility structure, the fund applies a research-driven, quant-enhanced investment strategy targeting momentum, technical patterns, and undervalued stocks. Built for performance and precision, Stonedge Capital represents a new-age approach to active equity investing, blending discipline, data, and founder conviction.
